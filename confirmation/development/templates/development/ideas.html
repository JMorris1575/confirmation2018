Plans for this Section

Eventually I would like to have a section of the website where team members could gather ideas and build activities together. I am not nearly ready to do that yet. For now, I have to go through a rather technical process to create an activity and the pages that go with it.

For now, though, I'd like you to come up with ideas for candidate activities. They should be designed to be interesting, fairly quick and simple to do, and yet worth doing -- in other words candidates should be able to advance in some way by completing an activity. They may end up knowing more than they did before, but, more importantly, they could grow in faith, or in interest, or in their basic "buy-in" to the Catholic faith.

Use the comment area in the section below to propose and discuss activity ideas. If I can, I will implement them for use by the candidates before opening the website to the whole group.
